BeInspired...

Connecting youths all over Nigeria.

We-Inspire Naija is an NGO, built to promote the lives of youth, men and women in the society to the glory of God.

We-Inspire Naija has three platforms

  1. MWG (Make We Gist)
  2. Inspired Sound
  3. Ket Initiatives

Since launching in 2019, We-Inspire Naija estimates it is responsible for matching tens of thousands of youths with each other. With hundreds of new members joining every month, you'll be sure to find the right person for your talents and be well on your way to meeting new friends.

Because We-Inspire Naija was created by musicians, you can be assured that we are constantly working to make sure this site is relevant to our user.
